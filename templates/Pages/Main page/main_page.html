What's a brochure website?

A brochure website is essentially what its name suggests - a digital version of a printed brochure. It's like a digital business card but with more functionality and potential. It shows the products or services your company provides, however, unlike an e-commerce website, it does not sell the product or service online. On a brochure website, the goods and services are only displayed, so prospective clients will have to contact the company to place an order or arrange a service.

For example, a small plumbing company would build a brochure website with a homepage to display contact information, an ‘about us’ page describing the company, and maybe a few photos of their work. There are a lot of good website builders out there that provide an off the box solution, but if you don’t have time to create a website yourself or want to get a more tailored solution than the website builders provide, then why not contacting me today outlining your needs to get a quote?
