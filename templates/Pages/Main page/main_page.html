What's a brochure website?

A brochure website is essentially what its name suggests - a digital version of a printed brochure. It's like a digital business card. It shows the products or services your company provides, however, unlike an e-commerce website, it does not sell the product or service online. On a brochure website, the goods and services are only displayed, so prospective clients will have to contact the company to place an order or arrange a service.

For example, a small plumbing company would build a brochure website with a homepage to display contact information, an ‘about us’ page describing the company, and maybe a few photos of their work. There is a lot of good website builders out there that provide an outside the box solution, read my blog on why you would and wouldn't want using these to build your website. If you don’t have time to create a website yourself or want to get a more tailored solution than the website builders provide, then why not contacting me today outlining your needs to get a quote?

Why would you want one?

  • It shows the products or services your company provides to a wider audience.
  • It's great for promoting your business.
  • Have more constructive conversations with your customers via web forms.
  • Increase your competitiveness.
  • Your prospective clients/customers may expect you to have your website so that they can find more information about your business.
  • Influence your brand perception by writing your own story on the website.
  • Show to others that you're serious about your small business. It adds credibility to your company.
  • By using effective words and captivating images, it can draw people in and help to sell your products.

What's possible?

  • The website can be responsive - be accessed on desktops, smartphones, and tablets through any browser, so you can be sure your company is reaching the maximum number of people.
  • The website can be bi-lingual. You won't need to provide me with the content in another language unless this language is other than English or Russian.
  • Almost everything is customisable - layout, colours, navigation bars, element shapes, buttons, forms, input fields, font style, types of pages, their numbers and structure. Make it your own.
  • Forms with any input types. Decide on what information you want to know about your customers.
  • A booking system. Let the automation do the work.
  • An email notification along with the form data from a new form submitted for you and a follow-up email for your prospective client. Make it easy for yourself while providing excellent customer service.
  • Dynamic testimonial board. Show what other people think about your product or service.
  • Simple animations. Have an eye-catching website, which is a bit more than a collection of monolithic pages.

Что такое брошюрный вебсайт?

Брошюрный вебсайт - это, по сути, то, о чем его название подсказывавет - цифровая версия печатной брошюры. Это как цифровая визитка. Однако он рекламирует продукты или услуги, которые предоставляет ваша компания, в отличие от Е-коммерс веб-сайта, он не продает товары или услуги в Интернете. На этом виде вебсайта только отображаются товары и услуги, поэтому потенциальным клиентам придется связаться с компанией, чтобы оформить заказ или организовать услугу. Например, небольшая сантехническая компания может создать брошюрный веб-сайт с домашней страницей для отображения контактной информации,страницей «о нас» с описанием компании и несколькими фотографиями их работ. Если у вас нет времени на создание веб-сайта самостоятельно, вы можете связаться со мной и заказать брошюрный вебсайт для вашего малого бизнеса сегодня.

  • Эти веб-сайты могут быть доступным на настольных компьютерах, смартфонах и планшетах через любой браузер, поэтому вы можете быть уверены, что вашу компанию достигает максимальное количество людей.
