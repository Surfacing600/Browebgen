STORY

DMITRY MARTINOV

Hi! I've decided to build this website to practice my basic knowledge in web development which I self-learned over 6 months period. I'm originally from Latvia but currently based in London. I've been living in London for over 10 years. During this time I got my BSc and MSc in Social Sciences before I landed my first professional job at an academic publisher. My flirting with programming languages begun in 2016 when I decided to take an introductory course in Python. I soon realised that while you can do amasing stuff with Python it's of little use to anyone without a user interface. During my current employment, I got exposed to some HTML and website building using Squarespace. I quickly understood how and what websites are built for and got interested in learning about how to create them myself. I treat my preoccupation with web development as my hobby at the moment, which perfectly fits with these typical rainy English days.

The website you're seeing is my first website built from scratch. I made decisions on everything you see here - colours, icons, logo, animations, structure, pages, font, layout and content. Regardless of which device you've accessed this website from it should look equally good as I've built it to be responsive. I thought that instead of me trying to figure out how to practise front end coding myself I can build websites for people who have small business and would like to harness the power of the Internet and get a wider outreach.

In case you're wondering why deer and why "BROWEBGEN", I'd like to demystify those two aspects of my logo. I like deers' habitat - mountains, deciduous forests, grasslands and rain forests. I also like how this animal looks because of their antlers, by the way, only males have them. Deer is athletic, which aligns with my predisposition towards doing sports and leading active lifestyle. The name of my brand reflects what services I provide - BROchure WEBsite GENerator (creator). I hope you'll get in touch and we'll take it from there!
